Tubay National High School

School is an institution for the teaching of children. Teachers teach children not only to read, write, and compute but also to teach them the values of godliness, patriotism, honesty, diligence, integrity, charity, as well as nurture them to discover and enhance their hidden talents and skills and to inculcate in their hearts and minds the aesthetic values for the fulfillment of their holistic development honed in the school. School profiling is essentially primordial in every institutional learning establishment to assess the success and failure in every endeavor on a program the school participates, Tubay National High School has this profile.



Our School

With the leadership of our principal of Tubay National High School Mrs. Ivy H. Beldad. The school offers grade 7 to grade 12 secondary years level with highly experienced and trained teachers. The Senior High School offers Academic Track like Humanities and Social Science (HUMSS) and Electrical Installation and Maintenance (EIM). Tubay National High School was formerly occupied by Tubay Central Elementary School-main which is now located at Tubay Lungsod Daan, Tubay Agusan Del Norte.
