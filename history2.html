History


Tubay National High School 55 years of existence, transcendentally braved the changing times. Its remarkable role as trainer and molder of significant people and heroes effortlessly.
Tubay National High School was established in 1969 which was located in Tubay Agusan Del Norte.
In Tubay National High School the one who donated was formerly Mayor Fidel E. Garcia Jr. in February 20 1990.
It was formerly occupied by the name of Tubay Nationalize which is now the name of the school was Tubay National High School.
In 1984 Jose P. Real Jr. was the first existed principal in Tubay National High School.
It was from the establishment of this institution to the present that we realized its success.
Tubay National High School won the Division Athletic Meet 2023 at Buenavista, Agusan del Norte last March 12-15, 2023.Tubay National High School in the upcoming Regional Athletic Meet 2023.
